Column owners. Title, DIV, SourcePath come from Import-MigrationSources.ps1. TotalSize + Size7YrMB/Size5YrMB/Size3YrMB + scan timestamp come from Invoke-UNCStorageScan-v2.ps1. TargetURL, TargetRelativePath, TeamChannelError, StorageQuota, StorageUsed, StorageAvailable, LastChecked come from Update-MigrationTargets.v2.ps1 (Phase 1 + Phase 2). ClaimedBy uses the format scan:DC.Server|target:DC.Server. Processing holds the atomic claim ID Processing-{computer}-{guid}. Operator sets Migrate=Stage or Migrate=Migrate and (optionally) TimeZone, Priority. Orchestrator writes Server, QueuedAt, StartDate, CompletedDate, LOG, ScriptError, YearUsed, Reacl, and the final Migrate state.
